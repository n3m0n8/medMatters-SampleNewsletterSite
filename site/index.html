The Mediterranean: Why it Matters.

The Mediterranean world is one of the oldest and most significant parts of the world; culturally, politically and economically. Despite the swift changes in global dynamics, the Mediterranean will remain a key element in the midst of alternating geopolitical power centres and conglomerations.

This website provides key news and insight into the region's key developments- taking the famous concept of the Mediterranean World outlined by French historian Fernand Braudel as the delimiting scope of coverage. This means that the countries covered include both those directly lining the sea's littoral, but so too is examining countries with core links to the region; such as the Sahel region, the Balkans, and the Arabian Gulf.

Mediterranean Matters offers insights and updates on key developments in the region. Please subscribe to the newsletter and check out the latest news, country profiles, and podcast using the navigation bar above.

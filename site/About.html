About Us

Mediterranean Matters is an independently run business-focussed newsletter bring the latest geopolitical and economic developments from the greater Mediterranean region. We provide key news covering countries in the European Mediterranean region (including the Balkans) as well as North Africa and the Levant.

Our coverage focusses on ongoing developments due to our nature as a newsletter, but our podcast and opinion pages take a longer, more historical and reflective view.

Our opinions appear fortnightly and feature researchers and voices relating to the region while the podcast appears once every month and we try to have expert academic or industry/policy-making voices expresses themselves on the region's past and present dynamics.
